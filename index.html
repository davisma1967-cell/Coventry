About

Local roots. Senior craft.

Coventry Systems is based in Travelers Rest, South Carolina — in the foothills of the Blue Ridge. We were founded by IT leaders with 25+ years of experience designing and running networks, securing infrastructure, and leading technology teams for organizations that depend on technology to operate.

We started Coventry Systems because we kept seeing the same gap: small and mid-sized South Carolina businesses paying for either over-priced enterprise consulting or one-person IT shops that disappear when the work gets hard. We're built to be the alternative — senior-level capability, delivered with the responsiveness of a local partner.
